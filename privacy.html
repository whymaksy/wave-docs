Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Wave" mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application"), developed by an independent developer, collects, uses, and protects your information. We are fully committed to protecting your privacy and security through Zero-Knowledge and End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) architecture.

1. Age Appropriateness

The Application is intended for a general audience and has no age restrictions. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate context, as our data collection is minimized to the lowest technical limit.

2. Data Collection and Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Our infrastructure is designed so that we have no access to your private life, goals, or financial records.

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data created within the Application—including but not limited to goals, tasks, financial histories, balances, and custom titles—is automatically encrypted directly on your device before being transmitted to the cloud synchronization servers.
  • Cryptographic Standards: The Application utilizes the Argon2id key derivation function based on your master password combined with the AES-GCM (256-bit) encryption algorithm.
  • Unencrypted Data: The only piece of information visible to the server infrastructure in plain text is your username, which is required for account identification, global uniqueness checks, and basic system interactions.
  • No Master-Password Storage: Your master password is never sent to the server or stored in plain text anywhere. We have zero technical ability to decrypt or read your data.

3. Third-Party Analytics and Tracking

We value a clean and tracker-free user experience. The Application does not use any third-party analytics software, tracking tools, advertisement networks, or error-reporting crash trackers (such as Firebase Crashlytics, Google Analytics, or Amplitude). Your behavior inside the Application is entirely private.

4. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We believe that you should have absolute control over your information.

Instant Deletion Policy: Once you request account deletion within the Application settings and confirm your action, acknowledging that the process is irreversible, all of your synchronized data, encrypted payloads, username, and account credentials will be erased instantly and permanently from our active databases. We do not retain any delayed backups of your personal records.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you need assistance regarding your account, you can contact us directly via the Help section inside the Application, or through the following official channels:
